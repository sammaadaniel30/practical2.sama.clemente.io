Fundamentals of Computer Science as a Subject
It is one of the most important initial subjects. Below is a brief introduction to what this subject teaches.

The course of The Fundamentals of Computer Science provides students with a comprehensive vision of the discipline, from its theoretical foundations to the practical skills necessary for professional practice.

In the 1st part, the course will address the theoretical foundations of computing, including mathematics, physics, anthropology and its ethics. In the 2nd part, the course will focus on practical skills, such as the representation and organization of information, the processing, management and transmission of information, and web development.

In addition to providing the necessary technical knowledge, the course will also contriubute to the development of soft skills, such as teamwork, ethical commitment and technical communication.
