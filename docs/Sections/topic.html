3D PRINTING (SPACESHIPS AND SATELLITES)

Our group did an investigation on 3D priniting applied to (Spaceships and Satellites). We found out that right now there is one company in the world so far, which is only specilized in the creation and manufacturing of 3D printed component in this sector.

This company is called Relativity Space, which created and launched the Terran 1 (see image). The first rocket manufactured with 3D pritedcomponents.

We concluded that this technology is rather new and so far it is quite challeging but the vision and future of this technology seems rather promising.
